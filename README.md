# Drehabwen

I build practical AI systems for rehabilitation, clinical workflow, and medical research.

The current work centers on **DeepRehab / Drehab**: tools that connect movement screening, pediatric spine-health follow-up, medical documentation, research reading, and AI-assisted production workflows.

## Focus

- Rehabilitation screening and movement analysis
- Pediatric spine-health and family follow-up tools
- Medical voice-to-record workflows
- Medical research assistants for literature and writing
- Codex skills and AI production pipelines

## Direction

My long-term direction is to make rehabilitation and medical AI more operational:

```text
screening data
-> clinical reasoning support
-> family-side execution
-> research evidence
-> reproducible AI workflows
```

The goal is not to replace clinicians, but to build tools that help clinical teams and families capture better information, follow through on plans, and communicate more clearly.
